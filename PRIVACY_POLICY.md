# Privacy Policy — Readcraftery

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Applies to:** Readcraftery (PC, Web, Android, iOS)

---

## The short version

Readcraftery does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

---

## What data we collect

**None.**

Readcraftery does not collect:
- Names, email addresses, or any account information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports sent to external servers
- Any information about the player

---

## Where game data is stored

All game data — progress, stars, settings, reading journal — is saved locally on the device running the game, in the standard user data directory provided by the operating system. This data never leaves your device.

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the game or clearing its local data through your operating system or app store.

---

## Children

Readcraftery is designed for children ages 4–9. Because we collect no personal data from anyone, the game is compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and equivalent regulations by design. There is nothing to collect, store, or share.

---

## Third-party services

Readcraftery does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

The game uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) system for read-aloud functionality. This is a local, on-device feature. No audio or text is sent to external servers.

---

## Community mods

If you install community-created Book Packs or Theme Packs, those files are loaded locally on your device. Readcraftery does not transmit mod content or your mod usage to any server.

Community mods are created by third parties. Readcraftery is not responsible for the content of community mods. Install mods only from sources you trust.

---

## Distribution platforms

When you purchase or download Readcraftery through itch.io, Steam, Google Play, or the Apple App Store, those platforms may collect their own data as part of the transaction. That data is governed by their respective privacy policies, not this one.

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the game adds optional features that involve data — this document will be updated and the "Last updated" date will reflect the change. Any future data collection would be clearly disclosed and opt-in.
