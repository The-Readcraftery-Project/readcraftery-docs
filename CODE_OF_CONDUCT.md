# Code of Conduct — The Readcraftery Project

## Our commitment

The Readcraftery Project is a community built around a game for children. We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone — regardless of age, background, experience level, gender, nationality, or any other characteristic.

This Code of Conduct applies to all public spaces of The Readcraftery Project: GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and any community spaces associated with the project.

---

## Our standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Show empathy toward other community members
- Credit others' work and ideas appropriately

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any content that sexualizes, endangers, or demeans children
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Deliberate intimidation or trolling

---

## Content standards for mods and contributions

Because Readcraftery is designed for children ages 4–9, all contributed content — example book packs, themes, documentation — must be appropriate for that audience.

Contributions containing violence, sexual content, hate speech, or any material inappropriate for young children will be removed without discussion.
